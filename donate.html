Why Your Support Matters

Jasper Samuel serves as the founding pastor of Kingship Christian Centre. Giving supports the church’s vision for worship, teaching, family restoration, leadership development, community outreach and practical care.

Every contribution helps the ministry create spaces where people can encounter God, receive guidance, rebuild their lives and take part in meaningful service.

  • Support community outreach and compassionate relief.
  • Strengthen marriage, family and emotional-care initiatives.
  • Help fund church gatherings, teaching and discipleship.
  • Equip youth through mentorship, creativity and leadership development.

Open Hands Program

Where Love Takes Action

At Kingship Christian Centre, faith is not meant to remain in words alone. Through Open Hands, compassion becomes visible in practical care, consistent presence and relationships that restore dignity.

Education and Youth Support

School supplies, uniforms, mentorship, life-skills development and career guidance help young people participate fully in education and approach the future with greater confidence.

Family Strengthening

Marriage preparation, relationship workshops, parenting support and family guidance create safe spaces where communication can improve and trust can begin to return.

Emotional Care and Healing

Peer support and compassionate listening give people room to process grief, trauma, stress and anxiety without performance or judgement.

Community Relief

Seasonal outreach provides practical items such as clothing, blankets and hygiene support while reminding people that they are seen and valued.

Creative and Leadership Development

Music, writing, performance, mentorship and leadership opportunities help young people discover their voice, discipline and sense of purpose.
