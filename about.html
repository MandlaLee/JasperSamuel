About Jasper Samuel

A life shaped by faith, perseverance, family, ministry, writing and a commitment to helping people grow.

Pastor · Author · Mentor

Helping People Build Faith, Heal and Live with Purpose

Jasper Samuel is a pastor, husband, father, entrepreneur, author and mentor devoted to helping individuals and families pursue spiritual growth, emotional healing, healthy covenant relationships and kingdom-centred purpose.

Through prophetic and apostolic ministry, counselling, writing, leadership development and lived experience, he helps people navigate emotional wounds, relationship struggles, seasons of waiting and personal transformation.

He serves as the founding pastor of Kingship Christian Centre in Midrand, where the ministry focuses on biblical identity, healing, leadership, worship, family strengthening and practical community impact.

Early Faith and Calling

Jasper found Christ at an early age and walked with God throughout his childhood. He began pastoring during his school years and learned music as part of his spiritual formation.

A Journey from Nigeria to South Africa

Born in Nigeria, he relocated to South Africa in 2011 in response to a growing spiritual calling. The move laid foundations for his ministry, counselling and leadership work.

Serving and Growing in Ministry

While in East London, Jasper served at Global Faith Ministries, growing in leadership, counselling and discipleship before taking on wider pastoral responsibilities.

Marriage, Family and Healing

His experiences as a husband and father deepened his understanding of covenant, communication, family dynamics and the patient work of emotional restoration.

Kingship Christian Centre

In 2026, he founded Kingship Christian Centre in Midrand with a vision centred on transformation, biblical truth, spiritual identity, healing, worship and leadership development.

Writing and Mentorship

His books and teaching combine biblical principles, pastoral wisdom and emotional insight to guide readers through relationships, forgiveness, faith, leadership and personal growth.

Perseverance Through Hardship

Financial strain, closed doors and seasons of waiting taught him to trust God’s timing and see delay as a place of character formation rather than abandonment.

Kingdom Principles in Business

Jasper approaches work as stewardship, emphasising integrity, servant leadership and the use of marketplace influence as a platform for transformation.
