About Jasper Samuel

Pastor • Author • Mentor • Leader

Who He Is

J.O.S Ohaeri is a pastor, husband, father, entrepreneur, and author devoted to helping individuals and families pursue spiritual growth, emotional healing, and healthy covenant relationships. Through years of ministry, writing, counseling, and real-life experience, he has developed a deep understanding of the struggles many face in their personal lives, marriages, and spiritual journeys.

Early Life and Calling

Born in Nigeria, he relocated to South Africa in 2011 in response to a growing spiritual calling. He served under Global Faith Ministries in East London under Pastor Heart, where he honed his leadership and pastoral skills, focusing on counseling and discipleship.

Ministry Development

In 2019, he moved to Bloemfontein and opened a branch ministry while simultaneously building family and business foundations. Married during this period, he welcomed his first daughter in 2020 and first son in 2023. These experiences strengthened his insight into family dynamics and relational counseling.

Focus and Passion

Years of pastoral counseling, ministry experience, and personal growth inspired a deeper passion for marriage restoration, emotional maturity, biblical leadership, and relational healing. His work equips individuals and families to navigate communication challenges, conflict, emotional wounds, forgiveness, and restoration.

Kingship Christian Centre

In 2026, Jasper Samuel founded Kingship Christian Centre in Pretoria, a ministry centered on spiritual identity, transformation, biblical truth, leadership development, healing, and kingdom living. The ministry equips believers to embrace their God-given identity, develop spiritual maturity, and live intentionally.

Writing and Teaching

Alongside ministry, he writes books focused on relationships, emotional growth, faith, purpose, and personal transformation. His writing blends biblical principles, pastoral wisdom, and practical guidance to help readers navigate real-life challenges and build healthier relationships.

Life Mission

Jasper Samuel seeks to encourage individuals and families toward stronger relationships, deeper self-awareness, spiritual maturity, emotional healing, and intentional covenant living. He believes that truth, humility, accountability, grace, and intentional love are essential for restoring marriages and relationships.
