About Jasper Samuel

ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Pastor, Author, Mentor, and Kingdom Leader

J.O.S Ohaeri is a pastor, husband, father, entrepreneur, and author devoted to helping individuals and families pursue spiritual growth, emotional healing, and healthy covenant relationships.

Through years of ministry, counseling, writing, leadership development, and real-life experience, he has developed a passion for helping people navigate emotional wounds, relationship struggles, spiritual growth, and personal transformation.

Early Life & Calling

Born in Nigeria, Jasper Samuel relocated to South Africa in 2011 in response to a growing spiritual calling. He later served faithfully under Global Faith Ministries in East London, developing in leadership, counseling, and discipleship.

Marriage & Family

In 2019, he moved to Bloemfontein, where he opened a branch ministry while simultaneously building family and business foundations. His experiences as a husband and father deepened his understanding of family dynamics, covenant relationships, and emotional healing.

Kingship Christian Centre

In 2026, he founded Kingship Christian Centre in Pretoria — a ministry focused on identity, transformation, biblical truth, leadership development, healing, and kingdom living.

Writing & Teaching

His books combine biblical principles, pastoral wisdom, emotional insight, and practical guidance to help readers navigate communication, relationships, forgiveness, healing, and personal growth.
