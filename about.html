About Jasper Samuel

Pastor, Author, Mentor, and Advocate for Healing, Growth, and Kingdom Living

Who He Is

J.O.S Ohaeri is a pastor, husband, father, entrepreneur, and author devoted to helping individuals and families pursue spiritual growth, emotional healing, and healthy covenant relationships. Through years of ministry, writing, counseling, and real-life experience, he has developed a deep understanding of the struggles many face in their personal lives, marriages, and spiritual journeys.

Early Life and Calling

Born in Nigeria, J.O.S Ohaeri later relocated to South Africa in 2011 in response to a growing sense of spiritual calling and purpose. Upon arriving in South Africa, he faithfully served under Global Faith Ministries in East London under the leadership of Pastor Heart. During this time, he further developed his skills in pastoral care, ministry leadership, preaching, and relational counseling.

Ministry Development

In 2019, he moved to Bloemfontein, where he established a branch ministry while simultaneously focusing on family life and entrepreneurship. During this season, he got married and began building both a family and a business alongside his wife. Their first daughter was born in 2020, followed by their first son in 2023. This period of personal and professional growth helped him gain invaluable experience in balancing ministry, business, and family.

Focus and Passion

Years of pastoral counseling, family leadership, ministry experience, personal growth, and exposure to emotional struggles inspired a deeper passion for marriage restoration, emotional maturity, biblical leadership, and relational healing. His work seeks to equip individuals and families with the tools to navigate communication challenges, conflict, emotional wounds, forgiveness, and restoration.

Kingship Christian Centre

In 2026, J.O.S Ohaeri relocated to Pretoria and founded Kingship Christian Centre, a ministry centered on spiritual identity, transformation, biblical truth, leadership development, healing, and kingdom living. The ministry focuses on guiding believers to embrace their identity in Christ, develop spiritual and emotional maturity, and engage in intentional covenant relationships.

Writing and Teaching

Alongside ministry, Jasper Samuel has dedicated himself to writing books focused on relationships, emotional growth, faith, purpose, and personal transformation. His writing combines biblical principles, pastoral wisdom, relational insight, and psychologically informed concepts to help readers navigate real-life challenges. He believes many marriages and relationships can be restored when truth, humility, accountability, grace, and intentional love are allowed to guide the process.

Life Mission

Through his ministry and writing, Jasper Samuel seeks to encourage individuals and families toward stronger relationships, deeper self-awareness, spiritual maturity, emotional healing, and intentional covenant living. His mission is to provide tools, resources, and guidance that empower people to overcome challenges, heal emotional wounds, and live a life of purpose, restoration, and faith.

Personal Philosophy

Jasper Samuel firmly believes that personal growth and spiritual development are intertwined. He encourages believers to pursue God wholeheartedly while also taking responsibility for their emotional health, relationships, and life decisions. Through his books, teachings, and pastoral care, he provides a holistic approach to living a balanced, joyful, and kingdom-centered life.

Get Connected

To learn more about his ministry, upcoming events, or books, visitors are encouraged to reach out, subscribe for updates, or participate in programs and teachings offered by Kingship Christian Centre. Every interaction is designed to uplift, restore, and inspire a deeper walk with God.
