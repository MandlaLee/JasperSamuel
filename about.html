About Jasper Samuel

ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Pastor, Author, Mentor, and Kingdom Leader

Jasper Samuel is a pastor, husband, father, entrepreneur, and author devoted to helping individuals and families pursue spiritual growth, emotional healing, and healthy covenant relationships.

Through years of ministry, counseling, writing, leadership development, and real-life experience, he has developed a passion for helping people navigate emotional wounds, relationship struggles, spiritual growth, and personal transformation.

Early Life, Faith & Calling

Jasper Samuel found Christ at an early age and walked with God throughout his entire childhood. He pastored during his school years and learned music as part of his spiritual development. Born in dNigeria, he later relocated to South Africa in 2011 in response to a growing spiritual calling. This move laid the foundation for his future leadership, counseling, and discipleship development.

Serving in Global Faith Ministries

While in East London, Jasper served faithfully Global Faith Ministries, where he grew in leadership, counseling, and discipleship. This season equipped him for pastoral and ministry responsibilities that would later shape his life’s work.

Marriage, Family & Emotional Healing

In 2019, he moved to Bloemfontein, opening a branch of Global Faith ministry while building family and business foundations. His experiences as a husband and father eepened his understanding of family dynamics, covenant relationships, and emotional healing.

Kingship Christian Centre & Writing Ministry

In 2026, he founded Kingship Christian Centre in Midrand, focused on identity, transformation, biblical truth, leadership development, healing, and kingdom living. His books combine biblical principles, pastoral wisdom, and emotional insight to guide readers in relationships, forgiveness, and personal growth. forgiveness, and personal growth.

Hardships Before Business & Patience in God

Before his business ventures gained traction, Jasper faced significant setbacks, financial strain, closed doors, and seasons of waiting. Yet through prayer and perseverance, he learned to trust God’s timing. This patience became a cornerstone, teaching him that delays are not denials but opportunities for character growth and divine preparation.

Business

Jasper integrates kingdom principles into his business endeavors, viewing work as worship and stewardship. Whether in ministry operations, publishing, or other ventures, he emphasizes integrity, servant leadership, and using marketplace influence to advance God’s purposes. His approach demonstrates that business can be a platform for transformation, not just profit.
