ABOUT JASPER SAMUEL

Pastor, Author, Mentor, and Kingdom Leader

Jasper Samuel is a pastor, husband, father, entrepreneur, author, and mentor devoted to helping individuals and families pursue spiritual growth, emotional healing, healthy covenant relationships, and kingdom-centered purpose.

Through ministry, counselling, writing, leadership development, and real-life experience, he helps people navigate emotional wounds, family struggles, spiritual growth, and personal transformation.
