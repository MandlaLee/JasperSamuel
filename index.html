About Jasper Samuel

Pastor, Author, Mentor and Kingdom Leader

Jasper Samuel is a husband, father, pastor, entrepreneur, author and mentor devoted to helping individuals and families pursue spiritual growth, emotional healing, healthy covenant relationships and kingdom-centred purpose.

As founding pastor of Kingship Christian Centre, he combines biblical teaching, pastoral wisdom, counselling and lived experience to help people navigate real challenges without losing faith or identity.
